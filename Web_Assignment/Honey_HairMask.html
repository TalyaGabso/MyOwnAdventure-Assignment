Coconut Oil and Honey Hair Mask

You will need:

1/2 Cup of Honey

3 Tablespoon of Coconut Oil


Directions:

If you have hard coconut oil soften it first and combine the honey and coconut oil.

Once your mask is ready, apply this mixture to yout hair after shampooing.

Leave it on for 20 minutes and rinse it out.
