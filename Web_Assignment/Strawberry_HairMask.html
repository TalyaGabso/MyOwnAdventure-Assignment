Strawberry Hair Mask

You will need:

5/6 Large Strawberries

1 Egg

1 Tablespoon of Honey


Directions:

Mash the strawberries as smooth as possible and add the egg and honey.

Once your mask is ready, apply this mixture to wet or dry hair.

Leave it on for 20 minutes and rinse it out.
