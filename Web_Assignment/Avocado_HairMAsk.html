Avocado Hair Mask

You will need:

1 Avocado

1 Egg

1 Tablespoon of Honey

1 Tablespoon of Coconut Oil


Directions:

Mix all of your ingredients together making sure to mash the avocado as smooth as possible.

Once your mask is ready, apply this mixture after shampooing.

Leave it on for 15/20 minutes and rinse it out.
