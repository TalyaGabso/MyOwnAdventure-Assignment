Olive Oil and Bananas Hair Mask

You will need:

1 Ripe Banana

1 Tablespoon of Olive Oil


Directions:

Mash the Banana as smooth as possible and add the honey.

Once your mask is ready, apply this mixture to damp hair.

Leave it on for 30 minutes and rinse it out.
